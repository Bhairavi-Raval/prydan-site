Meet Our Founder & CEO

Amit Kumar Trivedi, the CEO of PRYDAN Consultancy Services Pvt. Ltd., is a pioneering force in the staffing industry, boasting over two decades of expertise in technology, HR consulting, and organizational development. His strategic leadership has propelled PRYDAN to unparalleled growth, establishing it as a leader in staffing solutions. Amit's career spans various leadership roles in technology, staffing, and HR sectors, where he excelled in scaling operations and nurturing client relationships.

Under Amit's guidance, PRYDAN has set ambitious goals, aiming to fill 1 million positions by 2025 through the integration of cutting-edge technologies and innovative approaches. Beyond his professional endeavors, Amit's commitment extends to community building, evidenced by his founding of the IT Sales and HR Professional Communities, fostering a culture of learning and growth within the professional ecosystem. With a Master’s in Computer Application, Amit combines strategic foresight with operational excellence, driven by a passion to make a positive impact in the staffing industry and beyond.
